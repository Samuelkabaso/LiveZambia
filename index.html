A landlord account and a tenant account on the LIVE home rental app serve different purposes, offering unique features tailored to their roles in the rental process. Here’s how they differ:

Landlord Account
A landlord account is designed for property owners or managers who want to list their rental spaces and connect with potential tenants. Key Features:

Property Listings – Create and manage multiple rental listings with photos, descriptions, and pricing.

Booking Management – Accept, reject, or schedule viewings for rental requests.

Rent Collection – Set up payment methods and receive rent securely within the app.

Tenant Communication – Use in-app chat to answer inquiries and finalize rental agreements.

Reviews & Ratings – Receive feedback from tenants to build credibility.

Analytics & Reports – Track occupancy rates, payments, and rental performance.

Tenant Account
A tenant account is designed for users looking for rental properties that match their needs and budget. Key Features:

Search & Filters – Browse available properties based on price, location, and amenities.

Instant Booking – Apply for rentals, make secure payments, and schedule viewings.

Landlord Messaging – Communicate with landlords to ask questions or negotiate terms.

Saved Favorites – Bookmark favorite properties for easy comparison.

Rental History – Keep track of past and current rentals within the app.

Reviews & Ratings – Leave feedback on rented properties and landlords.

Both accounts offer a seamless experience, ensuring renters find the right home while landlords efficiently manage their properties.
